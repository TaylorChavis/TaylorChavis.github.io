Website Evaluations

Good Website: Crumbl Cookies

Description: Crumbl cookies is a brick and morter bakery shop that specializes in creative cookie desserts.

  • Contrast:
    • The text has a clear contrast from its background and makes it easy to read.
    • Buttons are distinct and navigation bar is organized.
    • There is a hover function that corresponds with the color of each cookie.
  • Repetition:
    • The header remains the same throughout the entire website
  • Alignment:
    • Products are evenly spaced out
  • Proximity:
    • The weekly cookie flavors are listed on the main page and grouped together for easy viewing.
    • White space is used to separate different sections

Conclusion: Crumbl cookies reflects the CRAP principles due to its clean and visually engaging design.It has high quality images, a consistant color scheme, and organized navigation. It creates an appealing user experience that highlights their menu, merch, and online ordering.

Bad Website: Penny Juice

Description: Penny Juice is an online store for selling 100% blended fruit juice for childcare centers.

  • Contrast:
    • The site uses clashing neon colors and inconsistent fonts
    • There is no navigation bar to efficiently maneuver around the website.
  • Repetition:
    • The header is too big and isnt connected to the top of the website. It feels like a banner that is blocking the view.
    • The order page is incredibly overwhelming and confusing to navigate
  • Alignment:
    • The site does not conform to mobile or split screen views.
    • The top and bottom margins could be improved upon.
  • Proximity:
    • no clear product page.
    • The layout is cluttered and poorly organized. Text and images are crammed together.

Conclusion: Penny Juice does not implement the CRAP web design in any meaningful way. The site is overwhelming, outdated, and overall feels unprofessional which reduces trust in the product. The design is not mobile friendly and lacks user interface standards. Overall, the site is very poorly designed and could benefit from completely starting over.
