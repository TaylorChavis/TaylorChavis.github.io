Requirements & Design Proposal

Project Overview

  1. My application is a photography website that showcases my clients photography business. It displays his background, the services he provides, contact information with a submittable form, and his portfolio through clickable images. It's purpose is to promote his business as an upcoming photographer and to broadcast his work.
  2. The intended users are people who are looking for photography services or anyone who would like to view his art. Services can vary from wedding photography, professional headshots, family portraits, or personal projects.
  3. The website will consist of 5 pages. The index will be welcoming people to the website and showing what it has to offer. The second page will be his about page and will explain his background and experience. The third page will be his portfolio and will provide interactive images of his work. The fourth page will be what services he can provide. Finally, the last page will be his contact page and will contain a submittable form for any service inquieries.
